Call for Papers

TAAI 2022 consists of both the international track and domestic track. Papers on academic researches and industrial applications of artificial intelligence and related areas are welcome. TAAI 2022 is open to international participants. We also welcome experienced scholars to organize special sessions to detail specific topics. Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into the IEEE Digital Library (IEL) and Scopus Database. Selected papers from the international track will be invited to submit an extended version for the special issue in the SCI Journals such as Journal of Information Science and Engineering (JSIE).


Topics of Interest

The conference will cover a wide range of research topics related to AI (Artificial Intelligence) and its applications. The topics of interests include but not limited to:

  • Autonomous Agents and Distributed AI
  • Computer Games
  • Computer Vision / Pattern Recognition
  • Data Mining and Knowledge Discovery
  • Federated Learning
  • Fuzzy Systems and Fuzzy Neural Networks
  • Genetic and Evolutionary Computation
  • Knowledge Representation and Automatic Reasoning
  • Knowledge-Based Systems
  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Problem Solving and Search
  • Recommender Systems
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Speech Recognition and Synthesis
  • Web Intelligence and Social Networks
  • Data Privacy and Ethics
  • Explainable AI
  • Human-in-the-loop AI
  • Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR)
  • Artificial Intelligence in Finance (FinTech and digital finance, Blockchain, etc.)
  • Artificial Intelligence in Medicine and Healthcare
  • Artificial Intelligence in Transportation, Urban Planning, Sustainability


Submission

International track: Papers for the international track have to be written and presented in English. Manuscripts should be submitted in PDF format and formatted using the IEEE 2-column format, including the bibliography within 6 pages. Submissions to TAAI 2022 are single-blind, i.e. the names affiliations of the authors should be listed in the submitted version.

Important information:

  • IEEE Manuscript Templates： Download
  • At least one author per accepted paper must register and attend the conference to present for the accepted submission to be included in the conference proceedings.
