Special Sessions S3

Technologies and Applications for Artificial Intelligence and Internet of Things (TAAIoT)

organizer ：Prof. Mengta Chung1, Prof. Che-Nan Kuo2, Prof. Yu-Da Lin3 and Prof. Yu-Huei Cheng4
Institution ：Tamkang University1, CTBC Financial Management College2, National Penghu University of Science and Technology3 and Chaoyang University of Technology4
E-mail ：157333@mail.tku.edu.tw1, fkikimo@hotmail.com2, yudalinemail@gms.npu.edu.tw3 and yuhuei.cheng@gmail.com4

The combination of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and the Internet of Things (IoTs), called AIoT has become the current and future trend. Today, IoT devices are ubiquitous. People are increasingly accustomed to the convenience and personalized services provided by IoT devices. Data are accumulated continuously, and a large amount of data promote AI feasible for reliable analysis. More and more industries began to introduce AI technologies. Discussing and developing the technologies and applications of AIoT will help promote the industry’s future progress. This special session aims to discuss any IoTs and AI-related knowledge and innovative applications of AIoT. Prospective authors are invited to submit original papers to the Special Session.

Topics/Areas

The scope of the special session TAAIoT 2022 includes, but is not limited to the following topics:

  1. AI Big data analysis
  2. Applications of industries using AIoT
  3. Control and Robotics on AIoT
  4. Data collection and analysis based on AIoT
  5. Image recognition by AIoT
  6. Innovative IoTs devices
  7. Intelligent Database Systems
  8. Machine learning methods
  9. Novel/Improved AI related algorithms
  10. Novel technologies development on AIoT
  11. Object detection technology based on AIoT
  12. Sensing technologies of IoTs
  13. Smart transformation applications by AIoT

Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into the IEEE Digital Library (IEL) and Scopus Database. Selected papers from the international track will be invited to submit an extended version for a special issue in the Journal of Information Science and Engineering (JSIE, indexed by SCIE) after the conference. Please note that papers must submit via the submission system website and meet the format of TAAI2022.
